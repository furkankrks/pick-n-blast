Privacy Policy for Pick 'N' Blast!

Effective date: July 14, 2026
Developer: Pick 'N' Blast! Development Team
Contact: supamechgames@outlook.com
Account deletion: Delete Account

Pick 'N' Blast! Development Team (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the Pick 'N' Blast! mobile game (the “Game”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, retain, and delete information when you use the Game and its online features.

Information We Collect

Online profile and sign-in information

When you use online features, we create or use a Supabase-backed game profile. We process a unique account or player identifier, your chosen username or nickname, and authentication session information needed to keep you signed in.

You can use online features with a guest or anonymous profile. If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive authentication information made available for sign-in, such as a Google authentication token, account identifier, and display name. We send required authentication information to Supabase to authenticate you and access your game profile. We do not request access to your Google contacts, files, photos, or other Google-account content.

If email/password sign-in is made available in a future version, we will also process the email address and authentication information required for that feature.

Online gameplay and user-generated content

If you use online features, we process question-pack titles, questions, answers, explanations, answer options, reports about online content, and leaderboard information. Leaderboard information includes username, score, level reached, deaths, revives, coins, and submission time. We also process moderation and anti-abuse records connected with online content.

Published question packs, usernames, and leaderboard entries may be visible to other players. Do not include personal, private, sensitive, or confidential information in online content.

Advertising and device information

When ads are enabled, the Game uses Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). AdMob automatically collects and shares IP address, which may be used to estimate approximate location; app interactions such as app launches, taps, ad views, and video views; diagnostic information such as app launch time, hang rate, and energy usage; and device/account identifiers such as Android Advertising ID and App Set ID, and where applicable identifiers related to signed-in accounts on the device.

Google uses this information for advertising, ad measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention. The Game may also store settings and sign-in session information locally on your device so that the Game and online features can function.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide gameplay, online packs, leaderboards, and sign-in; create and maintain online profiles; moderate user-generated content; prevent cheating, spam, fraud, abuse, and security incidents; show and measure ads when ads are enabled; respond to support, privacy, and deletion requests; and comply with legal obligations.

How Information Is Shared

We share information only as needed to operate the Game. Supabase provides authentication, database, and backend services. Google provides Google sign-in, Google Play services, and AdMob advertising services when those features are enabled. Other players can see public usernames, published question packs, and leaderboard information. We may disclose information where required by law or necessary to protect players, the Game, or the service.

Third-party privacy policies: Google Privacy Policy and Supabase Privacy Policy.

Advertising Choices and Consent

Where required by law, including in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we ask for consent before using personal data or device storage for advertising. You can change or withdraw advertising consent through Settings → Privacy Choices in the Game, where available, or use your Android device advertising settings.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to operate the Game, provide online features, maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Active online-profile information is retained while the profile remains active. Published packs and leaderboard information remain available until removed by you, deleted through an account-deletion request, or removed through moderation. Reports and moderation records may be retained when necessary to enforce rules, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

After a verified deletion request, we delete or anonymize account-linked personal data within 30 days, except information we must retain for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution. Backup copies may remain for up to 90 days before removal or overwriting.

Account and Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your active online account and associated data in the Game through Settings → Delete Account. You can also request deletion through our public account-deletion page after uninstalling the Game.

Deleting an account removes or anonymizes the online profile, question packs, leaderboard entries, reports, and associated account data, subject to the retention exceptions described above.

Security and International Processing

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. Communication with our backend and advertising providers uses encrypted connections where supported. No internet service or storage method is completely secure.

Our service providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. We take reasonable steps to use providers that apply appropriate safeguards to personal information.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required in their country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, contact us at supamechgames@outlook.com.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or restriction of processing of your personal information. You may also have the right to object to certain processing or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. To make a request, contact us at supamechgames@outlook.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Game, our data practices, or legal requirements change. We will post the updated version at this URL and revise the effective date.
